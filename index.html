Appearance

She is a beautiful well-endowed young woman who has blue eyes and short blonde hair that is white on top of her head.

As the 6th Unit Chief, she is often dressed in her Anti-Demon Corps uniform, consisting of a navy blue militia tunic, a cap with red highlights, white gloves, shorts, and thigh-high boots. She has worn two different sets of earrings, a single earring with a feather on her right ear and blue pendant earrings.

Tenka is often seen wearing civilian clothes when she isn't in service.

Personality

Tenka is a calm, mature, and good-natured woman, which was noted from her interactions around the 7th Unit, rarely showing anything but a lighthearted and genuine demeanor. Dissimilar from Yachiho, she is confident in her abilities and her unit, but not overconfident and never looks down on others like the latter. It is noted that Tenka refers to others affectionately, such as calling Kyouka "Kyou-chin" and Yuuki at first "Slave-kun".

Similar to Kyouka, the members of her unit look up to Tenka, and she, in turn, cares about them. She does not mind if any of them lose a battle, taking it as a lesson for them, giving them all advice after a loss. She will gladly jump in to save her subordinates' lives from an opponent.

She developed an initial interest in Yuuki, not looking down on him for being a man or slave. These feelings progressed as she witnessed him fighting and helping others, causing her to develop strong feelings for him. She is very brash and straightforward with her interest with him, attempting to seduce him after only meeting him recently. She often flirts with him alone or in front of others, even asking Kyouka if she could have Yuuki. In fact, though Tenka hadn't shown anger before, she hated the fact that Yuuki was kidnapped by the humanoid Shuuki led by his older sister. Her feelings were such that she couldn't bring herself to hurt Aoba since she knew how much she meant to Yuuki, instead taking a hit head-on. She is also not one to hold grudges, as her interactions with Aoba following their fight are shown to be amicable, even somewhat sisterly.

Background

Tenka has been an honor student from childhood, never failing in anything. She was never interested in boys her age, and lived life in boredom whenever alone, even after becoming a member of the Anti-Demon Corps.

Powers and Abilities

Overview

Like all other women, Tenka attained a unique power having devoured one of the peaches from Mato. As one of the Anti-Demon Corps' Chiefs, she is much more powerful than normal Demon Hunters and as such is one of the strongest women in the organization. Befitting the 6th Unit Chief, Tenka has the highest authority among the 6th Unit. As a testament to her strength, Tenka was capable of defeating a Shuuki horde alone, then fought against and overpowered one of the Eight Thunder Gods, Rairen almost killing him if Shikoku hadn't saved him.

Peach Blessing

Ame-no-Mitori (天御鳥命, Ame-no-Mitori, lit. "Heavenly Bird Life"): She is able to manipulate space and can teleport from one location to another. However, Tenka needs to touch someone else to teleport them. She can twist the space around her opponent ignoring all their defenses making it a sure kill move. She is capable of also teleporting around the battlefield 666 times without stopping.

Physical Abilities

Immense Strength: Tenka has impressive strength, being able to kick an opponent as strong and as massive as Rairen aside using a single attack.
